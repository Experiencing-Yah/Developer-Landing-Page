What this book is about

This book is a reframing of Matthew 5:38–42, where Yeshua brings clarity to the phrase “an eye for an eye and a tooth for a tooth.”

We explore the background of that law, Hebrew translations of Matthew, and cultural context to show how Yeshua is not teaching us to be submissive toward bullies—but how to apply this principle in everyday life, so we can be wise and take back our power when others try to steal it from us.
