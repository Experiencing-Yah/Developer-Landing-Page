BibliCal

Version 0.1.4

A moon-sighting-based Biblical calendar application for Android devices

About BibliCal

BibliCal is an Android app designed to help you track and manage a moon-sighting-based Biblical calendar system. The app provides tools to observe the Biblical calendar according to the traditional method of moon sighting, helping you stay connected to the Biblical timing system.

Biblical days begin at sunset, and months are determined by the sighting of the new moon. BibliCal assists you in tracking these important dates and making informed decisions about when months and years begin.

Features

Today Screen

View the current Biblical date (Month, Day, Year) at a glance

Calendar View

Browse projected months and see the full calendar layout

New Moon Sighting

Receive prompts on days 29/30 to confirm new moon sightings

Aviv Barley Prompt

Determine whether to add a 13th month or start a new year

Persistent Notification

Optional notification showing the current Biblical date

Home Screen Widgets

Add widgets to your home screen for quick access to the Biblical date

Calendar Export

Export Biblical month starts and feast days to your device calendar

Offline Functionality

Works completely offline - no internet connection required

Developer

BibliCal is developed by ExperiencingYah.com, dedicated to creating tools and resources that help people connect with God's word and follow Biblical principles in their daily lives.

Technology

BibliCal is built with modern Android development tools:

  • Kotlin - Modern programming language for Android
  • Jetpack Compose - Modern UI toolkit for building native Android apps
  • Room Database - For local data storage
  • DataStore - For preferences and settings

Privacy & Support

Your privacy is important to us. BibliCal stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers. For more details, please review our privacy policy.
