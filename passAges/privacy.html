PassAges Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

App Name: PassAges
Developer: ExperiencingYah.com
Platform: Android (iOS coming soon)

This privacy policy describes how PassAges ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your data. PassAges is a yearly Bible reading plan app aligned with weekly Torah readings, prophets, and Gospel events. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection and Usage

PassAges stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Reading progress and preferences (e.g. selected translation, reading plan options)
  • Downloaded Bible translation data (optional; stored locally after you choose to download)

No data is transmitted to external servers. The app may connect to the internet only to download optional Bible translations from our hosting; no personal data is sent.

No Analytics or Advertising

PassAges does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
