About PassAges

PassAges organizes yearly Bible reading around the weekly Torah passages used in traditional congregations. Each week you read from the Torah, the related prophet passage for that week, and Gospel passages that match the events that occurred in the week of the year.

You can stick to only the Torah, or you can add in the prophets, the full Tanakh, Gospels only, or include the rest of the Messianic Scriptures in the weekly plan so the whole Bible is read in a year alongside the traditional cycle.
