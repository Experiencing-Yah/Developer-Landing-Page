Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?
Yes. Bible texts and reading plans are stored locally. You only need internet for downloading additional translations or opening external resource links.
What reading plan options can I choose?
Torah is always included. Prophets can be related/full/none, and Messianic Writings can be Gospels/full/none. You can start “From the Beginning” or “Current Week.”
How do I change the current week?
Go to Settings and use the week picker. You can jump to any week manually.
What is the 13th month setting?
It controls whether the plan uses a 54‑week cycle (with a 13th month) or a 50‑week cycle (with combined portions). The app will prompt you if it’s not set.
What do the streaks mean?
They count completed weeks and completed rounds of the plan, not consecutive daily streaks.
How do I reset my progress?
In Settings, use “Reset all data” to clear completions and restart setup.
What translations are included by default?
WEB and WEB Hebrew Name are bundled. KJV and KJV Hebrew Name are available via download.
What is BibliCal integration?
If the BibliCal app is installed, you can pull the current week automatically from it (optional).
Why do resource links open in a browser?
Weekly and global resources are external links, so they open outside the app.
