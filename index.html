About

Experiencing Yah is dedicated to helping you experience a deeper relationship with the one true God. Through our apps, books, and resources, we provide tools and content designed to support your spiritual journey and understanding of God's word.

Our mission is to create resources that help people connect with God authentically, study Scripture with clarity, and grow in their faith without the influence of man-made traditions.

We utilize technology to give access to the Word of God and strengthen daily living for God. Our apps and partnerships provide tools that help you engage with Scripture and grow in your faith.
